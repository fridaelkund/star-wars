instructions

Here's how to play the game.

Create character

Create a character, with a name and awesome looks, and save him/her. If you want to create several characters, they are all saved for future use!


Fight the battle

Go to the battle field and conquer the galaxy. You chance to win a planet is based on how much you look like one of its habitants.

Hang out in your imperium

Go to your profile to see all your planets and view info about them.
